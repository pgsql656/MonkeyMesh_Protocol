The Paradigm Shift

Moving from a centralized server model to a decentralized agentic mesh fundamentally changes the internet's properties.

  • ✓ Privacy: Data stays local on Layer 1 (Brain).
  • ✓ Censorship Resistance: Relays (Nostr) prevent platform-wide bans.
  • ✓ User Control: Agents negotiate on your behalf, filtering noise and ads.

The Jungle Stack

MonkeyMesh is not a website; it is a protocol stack running locally on user devices. This architecture ensures sovereignty at every layer.

Layer 1: The Brain

Local Intelligence

Tech: Ollama / Llama-3 (8B) / TinyLlama

Runs locally. Filters feeds, detects scams, and acts as the user's digital bodyguard. No data leaves without permission.

Layer 2: The Transport

The Vines

Tech: Nostr + WebRTC

Censorship-resistant relay network. Broadcasts encrypted "Signals" for jobs and content availability.

Layer 3: The Ledger

The Bananas

Tech: Banano (Block Lattice) + Tezos

Banano handles instant micro-payments and anti-spam PoW. Tezos handles smart contracts, royalties, and NFTs.

Layer 4: The Storage

The Memory

Tech: IPFS (InterPlanetary File System)

Decentralized hosting for media, music, images, and social graphs. Content is pinned by the community.

Core Modules

💼

The Gig Hunter

Work finds you.

Your agent passively scans the Mesh for "Job Signals." If a 500 BAN gig matches your Skill Profile, it auto-replies with verified credentials.

🛡️

Social Mirror

Your feed, your rules.

Agents clone your Web2 posts to IPFS for safekeeping. Local LLMs scrub ads and rage-bait from your feed based on your personalized "Taste Profile."

🔊

SoundSystem

Direct-to-Fan streaming.

Artist agents upload to IPFS. Fan agents stream files. Payment streams (e.g., 0.01 BAN/sec) flow directly to the artist's wallet.
